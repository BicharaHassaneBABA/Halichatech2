About the Founder

Real name Bichara Hassane Baba alias Nino is a young committed and determined as to the accomplishment of his mission. His educational background is rich in color with an excellent note of Buletin. However, he did the kindergarten at the social center of Mongo before going to Mandelia to start with the primary school of CP1 until CM1 then return to Mongo to do the CM2 at the school center of Mongo . As for the secondary study, it was at the Lycee de Mongo that he started the 6th until Terminale in 2016 where he obtained a scientific baccalaureate from the D series. Indeed, the latter he was a child Parliament of the Guera from 2012 to 2016, Young Reporter at Radio Communautaire of Mongo from 2014 to 2016, presenter of the genie program on FM Radio Libert from 2016-2017, presenter of the Journal in French on Radio and Television Al-Aamiya of the International University of Africa in Khartoum in Sudan and also Secretary General of Rfi Khartoun Club and responsible for communication and external relations of AEFCOM. For this fact, he participated in several types of training sessions including training on the convention on the rights of the child, animation and reporting technique, computer training, training in editing software such as Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effevts...
